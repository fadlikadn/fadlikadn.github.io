About

I'm a software engineer and Scrum Master with 10+ years of professional experience, building full-stack products and cloud infrastructure. I've shipped software with international teams across Indonesia, Australia, Japan & Germany — a lifelong learner, problem solver, and motivator who likes work that has to be correct, resilient, and maintainable.

Today I'm on the founding team at @noscai in Hamburg, building ClinicOS — a healthcare management platform for medical practices in the DACH region — with TypeScript, Rust & Tauri. Outside of work I build small tools that matter to my community: a zakat calculator, fasting schedules, and browser extensions for Indonesian ZISWAF providers.
